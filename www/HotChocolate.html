Hot Chocolate

Back

Ingredient

  • 60 g dark chocolate/li>
  • 1 tablespoon of bitter cocoa
  • 40 cl of milk
  • 10 cl of liquid cream
  • 20g sugar

Preparation steps

  1. In a saucepan, pour the milk, cream and sugar. Boil the water.
  2. Off the heat, add the dark chocolate and cocoa powder, then whisk vigorously until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Pour immediately into cups or bowls.
